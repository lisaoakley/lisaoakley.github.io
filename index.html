About Me

I am a Math and Computer Science undergraduate at Northeastern University. I expect to graduate in July of 2018.
Professionally, I am interested in mathematically rigorous areas of computer science. My goal for the next few years is to learn more about machine learning and cybersecurity.
Though I have interest in security and machine learning, I also have some design background and enjoy UX design and product management. In my last co-op at Runkeeper, I was able to work closely with design team and watch their methods and decision-making process. I had the opportunity to give input into UX decisions and saw some of my suggestions in the wild, especially in our Apple Watch app.
My more immediate goals are to improve my development skills and get more hands-on experience with code through classes, outside projects, and co-ops to help facilitate my long-term plans. As an undergraduate, I am also looking to refine my interests. I'm constantly reading, googling, following compelling people, speaking with professionals, googling more, going to workshops and conferences, and trying on all sorts of hats.
Apart from CS, I enjoy performing and watching improv comedy, attempting to complete crossword puzzles, watching Parks and Rec, dancing, doing yoga, and running.
